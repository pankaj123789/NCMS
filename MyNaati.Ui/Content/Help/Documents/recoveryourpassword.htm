Recover your password

To reset a forgotten password you must know your NAATI number and the email address registered with myNAATI.

1.  Click Forgot your password? You can  recover it here>

2.  Enter your Customer Number, email address and the text from the CAPTCHA image

3.  Click Reset Password

The system generates a new password and sends an email with the new password to your email.

Note:  You will be prompted to change your password when you log into myNAATI.
