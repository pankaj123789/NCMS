Register with myNAATI

A NAATI member can easily register with myNAATI.

1.  Click the Register button, or the Become one now! link, to display the registration screen.

 

The myNAATI Registration screen is displayed

2.  Enter your Customer Number, email address (in both the email and confirm email fields) and the text from the CAPTCHA image

3.  Click Submit to register for myNAATI

Your login details will be sent to the email address you provided.

If the Customer number and the email you enter do not match those recorded in NAATI’s internal system (SAM), you will be prompted to contact NAATI.
