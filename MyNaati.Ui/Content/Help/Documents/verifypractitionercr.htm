Verify practitioner credentials

A member of the public can search for and verify the credentials for a specific practitioner. Enter the practitioner’s Customer number and search to retrieve a list of the practitioner’s credentials, their state and photo.

1.  Click Verify Practitioner Credentials

2.  Enter the Customer Number

3.  Click Search

All credentials, and the dates that the credentials are valid, are listed with State and photo if available.

Note: If a practitioner is deceased, their image will not appear and the message (This person is deceased) will appear beside the name.
