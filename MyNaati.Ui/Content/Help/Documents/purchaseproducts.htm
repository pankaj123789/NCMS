Purchase Products

A registered user can order products for post accreditation practice through myNAATI. Orders can be placed using the myNAATI order wizard or by partially completing the order online, printing the order form and posting the completed order form by mail.  You can specify a delivery address for the order.  The Order Reference Number will be printed at the top of your order.

To order a Post Credential Product:

1.  Log in to myNAATI

2.  Select the Products menu

or
Click Get Started on the Welcome to myNAATI Page

The Post Credential products page briefly describes each of the products. Click Frequently Asked Questions for details of the terms and conditions such as types of payment accepted and creating an order form.

3.  Select the product type

4.  Use the Cancel, Previous and Next buttons to move through the wizard or click on a completed step to review your entries.

5.  Click the up and down arrows to increase and decrease quantity ordered.

6.  To save changes made to a previously completed screen, e.g. quantities ordered, click the Next button

7.  Click Confirm or to confirm the order or Download Form to download the partially completed form for you to send with your payment

A unique reference number is allocated to the order

8.  Click Print to print your copy of the order

9.  Click OK to return to the main menu

Note: To order ID Cards online NAATI requires an up-to-date passport photo. If required, you will be prompted to download the form and send the completed form to NAATI with two witnessed passport photos.
