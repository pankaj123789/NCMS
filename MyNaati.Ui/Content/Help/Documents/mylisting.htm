My Listing

Select this option to update your listing details or, if you are not listed in the NAATI Practitioners Directory, list your name and credentials using myNAATI to pay via credit card.

To list your details in the directory:

1.  Select Practitioners Directory>My Listing

2.  Click the Review your personal details link to update your details

3.  Return to the Practitioners Directory>My Listing screen

4.  Click List Now

The List in Practitioner Directory wizard is displayed

5.  Use the Cancel, Previous and Next buttons to move through the wizard

6.  Click Confirm to confirm the payment and listing
